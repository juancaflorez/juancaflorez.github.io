Masters in design | Spring 2020

Date

Jun 16, 2020

Categories

Game design

A collection of four projects done during the spring semester of 2020 at the Royal Danish Academy, School of Design, Master of Game Design.
They include a quick prototype for the National Museum of Denmark, another prototype for the Danish institute against torture (DIGNITY). And two additional internal projects on art direction in collaboration with the bachelors' students of game design.
