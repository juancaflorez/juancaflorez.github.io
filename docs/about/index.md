Juan Florez is a technical artist living and working in Berlin, Germany. 
He studied Visual Game and Media Design (M.D.) at the Royal Danish Academy of Fine Arts in Copenhagen, Denmark, and Fine Arts (B.A.) at Los Andes University in Bogota, Colombia. 
He currently works as technical artist at Stratosphere Games GmbH in Berlin, Germany.
