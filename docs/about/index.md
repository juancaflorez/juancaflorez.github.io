# About

![Portrait](rsr/portrait.png){ align=right width="200"}
Juan Florez is a technical artist living and working in Berlin, Germany.
He studied Visual Game and Media Design (M.D.) at [The Royal Danish Academy, School of Design](https://royaldanishacademy.com/royal-danish-academy), in Copenhagen, Denmark. He also obtained a bachelor of Fine Arts (B.A.) from [Los Andes University](https://uniandes.edu.co/en) in Bogota, Colombia.
He currently works as technical artist at Stratosphere Games GmbH in Berlin, Germany.
